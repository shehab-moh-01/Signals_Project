Step 2: Downsampled Audio

The audio has been downsampled by taking every second sample.

Downsampling: \( x_{\text{downsampled}}[n] = x[n \cdot 2] \). Reduces sampling rate from 44.1 kHz to 22.05 kHz, lowering data rate.
