Step 4: Filtered Audio

The audio after applying a low-pass filter to reduce noise.

Butterworth Filter: \( |H(j\omega)| = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 + \left(\frac{\omega}{\omega_c}\right)^{2N}}}, \quad \omega_c = 2\pi f_c \). Attenuates frequencies above \( f_c = 1000 \, \text{Hz} \) (order \( N = 4 \)).
