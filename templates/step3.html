Step 3: Fourier Transform

The frequency spectrum of the audio, computed using FFT.

Discrete Fourier Transform: \( X[k] = \sum_{n=0}^{N-1} x[n] e^{-j \frac{2\pi k n}{N}} \). Converts time-domain signal to frequency domain.
